## How to read this folder

- **README.md** is the staged front door.
- **CLAIMS.md** is the authoritative inventory of promoted, weakened,
  and retracted claims, each with a kill criterion.
- **CHARACTERIZATION_REPORT.md** is the integrated description of what
  the binary does.
- **IS_AND_IS_NOT.md** is the scoped ledger of positive and negative
  statements.
- **JOURNEY_LOG.md** is the session-by-session narrative.
- **REPO_AGENT_FINDINGS.md** records seams or mismatches that should
  stay visible.
- **MANIFEST.tsv** is the SHA-256 accounting surface for mirrored
  artifact paths.

## Retractions and weakenings are first-class events

If a claim's kill criterion is met, the claim is retracted and remains
visible in [CLAIMS.md](CLAIMS.md). If a later session forces a narrower
reading, the weakening remains visible too. H2, Claim γ, and the
Session 7 weakening of `δ.3` are protected public artifacts.

## Scope fence

The binary implements a 380-vertex relaxation system on a
C3-symmetric graph and exposes twelve callable tasks plus a six-gate
self-evaluating campaign. Everything in this folder is scoped to those
receipted behaviours.

DM3 is not:

- an AI system
- a cognitive or spiritual architecture
- a transformer-created bistability
- a generally programmable computer
- a place for commercial language

## Reproducibility

The gate surface is deterministic up to `run_sec`, and the Session 7
smoke receipt promotes canonical SHA `9006df4ec02c8872...` as the
current default fingerprint on the `--steps 5` surface. The harmonic
task remains stochastic in selection but stable in distribution across
the promoted Session 7 dynamics battery.

The repo also keeps one explicit structural seam visible: the Session 7
engineer handover names standalone gate-layer directories
`S2_pinned / S4_airplane / S6_core`, while the local checkout exposes
the later harness tree without those exact directory names. That
mismatch is recorded in [REPO_AGENT_FINDINGS.md](REPO_AGENT_FINDINGS.md)
instead of being narrated away.

Session 8 adds further live seams: the Phase A final report and summary
files use 55 total-run accounting, while the original Phase A local
mirror contains 52 per-run receipt/log pairs; B3 refines κ rather than
supporting a universal `--steps`-invariant ratio story; and the
airplane-mode deviation affecting 40 reported receipts plus the
CPU/GPU-only thermal-gate patch remain visible.
