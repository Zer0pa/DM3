`daaaa84a052b60523bf9d63152f1154225abf119c279aa4b3aabf14487279672`)
implementing a 72,960-dimensional relaxation system on a fixed
380-vertex C3-symmetric graph. It exposes twelve callable tasks,
including `exp_r1_r4_campaign`, a self-evaluating six-gate campaign
that emits structured pass/fail verdicts about its own operating
regime.

## What the binary actually does

- Accepts twelve `--task` values including `harmonic`, `holography`,
  `interference`, `holographic_memory`, `exp_r1_r4_campaign`, `exp_i1`,
  `exp_i2`, `exp_h1_h2`, `exp_k2_scars`, `exp_k3_truth_sensor`,
  `resonance_r3`, and `resonance_v2`.
- Under `--task harmonic` at default parameters it is bistable with
  HIGH basin `(E≈88, Coh≈0.77)` and LOW basin `(E≈75, Coh≈0.88)`.
- Under `--task exp_r1_r4_campaign` at defaults it emits six gates:
  3 PASS `(EPSILON_CRIT, R4, WAKE_SLEEP_ALIGN)` and
  3 FAIL `(R1, R2, R3)`.
- Session 7 smoke fixes the default six-gate fingerprint at canonical
  SHA `9006df4ec02c8872...` on the `--steps 5` surface.
- `RandomAdj_v1.bin` flips **R1**; `RegionTags_v2.bin` flips **R2** and
  advances `claim_level` to `CL-1`.
- The 2×2 cross-control table is now closed at default `--steps 1`,
  with `RA+v2` reproducing a single canonical SHA across 3 replicates.
- The compound configuration
  `RandomAdj_v1.bin + RegionTags_v2.bin + --steps 50`
  flips **R1** and **R2** together and raises `r4.transfer_ratio` to
  `2.67826509475708`.
- On the SY-default surface, requested `--steps > 20` does not extend
  `operational_steps` above `10`; `R3` remains false on the exposed CLI.
- `exp_k2_scars` is the first receipted positive learning line in this
  project: `best_uplift` rises
  `0.010 → 0.075 → 0.273 → 1.324` across
  `--steps 1 → 5 → 10 → 20`, then overfits at `50`.
- Session 8 refines `exp_k2_scars`: `--steps 20` is a shoulder, the
  mapped baseline curve is a trimodal sawtooth with local maxima at
  `33`, `41`, and `49`, and the overfit cliff is localized to `49→50`.
- At `--steps 20`, both adjacency topology and region-tag partition
  affect `exp_k2_scars`; the RA+v2 cell gives zero learning.
- The τ lane confirms bit-exact ARM64 cross-platform `exp_k2_scars`
  KPIs across RM10 native Android and an Apple M1 Android ARM64
  emulator for the tested baseline values.
- `exp_k3_truth_sensor` and `resonance_r3` both do real internal work,
  but their decorative-control claims are narrow. B3 shows K3 is
  `--steps`-responsive in absolute values, while `resonance_r3`,
  `resonance_v2`, and `exp_i2` are `--steps`-decorative at `1` vs `20`.
