# History

The branch still carries two mined history families:

- governed SoC or Genesis history for control-lane doctrine
- later DM3 residue for asset, naming, and accelerator clues

What changed in this phase is not the history itself, but the branch boundary
drawn around it:

- the governed Genesis family remains the only branch-grade control family
- the later residue remains useful for bounded accelerator probing only
- the residue no longer gets to imply a bridge into the governed family unless a
  same-family surface is evidenced

Use history as clue generation, not silent promotion.
