- the honest primary battery surface is `F1` CPU control

romance to resolve. What it cannot do is mistake a separate residue accelerator
line for the main scientific instrument. The immediate engineering detour still
worth taking is one bounded diagnostic pass on the `F2` GPU outlier, because
that is the only unresolved accelerator question with a live callable surface.
