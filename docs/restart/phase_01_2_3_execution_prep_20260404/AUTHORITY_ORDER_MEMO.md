# Authority Order Memo

## Exact Phase Verdict

- `phase_outcome=PASS`
- `route_outcome=FAIL`
- The preserved bundled G2 family is retired on the current callable same-binary surface.
- The blocker is no longer "which same-binary route should we try?"; it is whether the historical bundled G2 residue belongs to a neighboring launcher generation or requires explicit redevelopment.

## Purpose

This memo freezes the lane hierarchy after Phase `01.2.3` closeout.
It exists to stop confusion between callability, governance, engineering
readiness, and present authority.

## Authority Order

1. Mac CPU remains the safest authority lane.
2. Historical RM10 Genesis parity remains the strongest phone-side comparison
   witness.
3. Fresh RM10 Genesis lanes remain the first governed phone lanes, but they are
   still non-sovereign.
4. Root and bundled hybrid runners remain residue and archaeology lanes.
5. RM10 GPU remains an engineering target only.
6. RM10 NPU remains feasibility-only.
7. Heterogeneous embodiment remains optional later and does not outrank
   single-lane governed evidence.

## Lane Definitions

| Lane | Canonical status | What it can justify | What it still cannot justify |
| ---- | ---------------- | ------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Mac CPU | `authority_status=sovereign`, `build_class=source_built`, `evidence_surface=witness_floor` | current authority language, witness-floor comparisons, governance debugging | automatic sovereignty for fresh RM10 lanes or any hybrid science claim by itself |
| historical RM10 Genesis parity | `authority_status=comparison_only`, `build_class=not_applicable`, `evidence_surface=comparison_only` | that an RM10 witness floor once matched Mac cleanly | present-tense parity for newer phone-local lanes |
| fresh RM10 SoC runtime | `authority_status=governed_non_sovereign`, `build_class=prebuilt_stub`, `evidence_surface=witness_floor` | real phone executability and explicit-hash comparison on the lane-local tuple | repaired canonical validation or source-built parity |
| fresh RM10 raw workspace | `authority_status=governed_non_sovereign`, `build_class=mixed_prebuilt_backed`, `evidence_surface=witness_floor` | real raw-workspace executability and bounded witness-floor engineering | Mac parity or source-built parity |
| root and bundled hybrid runners | `authority_status=archaeology_only`, `build_class=exploratory_compiled_residue`, `evidence_surface=archaeology` | smoke-level callability, archaeology, and blocker proof | living non-stub tasks, GPU parity, or promoted hybrid authority |
| RM10 GPU | `authority_status=engineering_only`, `evidence_surface=engineering_feasibility` until parity rules exist | bounded feasibility and later parity work on a named CPU reference observable | present-tense science authority or same-binary bundled `G2` recovery |
| RM10 NPU | `authority_status=feasibility_only`, `evidence_surface=engineering_feasibility` | bounded user-space reachability or assist-role feasibility | authority-bearing runtime claims |
| heterogeneous embodiment | `authority_status=engineering_only`, `evidence_surface=handoff` only after prerequisites | bounded embodiment testing with named handoffs | current canon or authority promotion |

## Current Lane Locks

- Fresh RM10 Genesis lanes remain governed but non-sovereign, with unresolved
  default-validator reconciliation and no source-built parity promotion.
- The retired same-binary bundled `G2` family cannot be reused as a reference
  observable for GPU, NPU, or heterogeneous work.
- Later-lane work must inherit the serious-run identity, telemetry, checkpoint,
  and Comet contracts when it becomes live.

## Comparison Rules

Every comparison memo or run ledger must state:

- source lane
- target lane
- named observable
- validation rule used
- `authority_status`
- `evidence_surface`
- `build_class`
- the exact blocker when parity or authority is not yet honest

## Prohibited Collapses

- Do not collapse fresh RM10 executability into authority.
- Do not collapse explicit-hash validation into repaired canonical validation.
- Do not collapse engineering targets into accepted science lanes.
- Do not collapse feasibility-only probing into runtime recovery.
- Do not collapse mixed-compute aspiration into present embodiment fact.
