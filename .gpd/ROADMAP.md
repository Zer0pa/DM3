# Roadmap: DM3 2026 Restart

## Overview

This roadmap takes DM3 from manifesto-driven narrative back to source-backed, falsifiable research. The 3D Double Meru is the primary scientific object. The computational field-computer reading is the leading live hypothesis about that object. The older governed Genesis / SoC line remains the witness floor and anti-delusion instrument, while the later hybrid residue is treated as evidence about the Double Meru rather than as the program center.

Fresh execution evidence from Phases 2 to 4 was captured early during the Phase
01 pass.
That evidence is real, but it does not waive the remaining acceptance gates.
