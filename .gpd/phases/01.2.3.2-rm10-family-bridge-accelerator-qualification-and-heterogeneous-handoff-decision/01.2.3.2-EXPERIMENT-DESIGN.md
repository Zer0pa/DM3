## Common Identity Packet

Every serious run or probe must retain:

- exact command
- exact working directory
- lane label and family label
- binary path and SHA-256
- asset hashes when assets are used
- pre/post battery snapshot
- pre/post thermal snapshot
- stdout and stderr
- mirrored output receipt files
- checkpoint label

## Decision Rules

- `F2 stable feasibility` requires same-schema CPU/GPU receipts across both `A` and `B` pairs plus lane-specific stdout markers
- `bridge_candidate` requires an accelerator-relevant callable surface on `F1`; without that the bridge is closed regardless of `F2`
- `NPU PASS` requires a named callable user-space assist role with receiptable inputs and outputs
- `explicit heterogeneous PASS` is unavailable unless a same-family handoff artifact appears
