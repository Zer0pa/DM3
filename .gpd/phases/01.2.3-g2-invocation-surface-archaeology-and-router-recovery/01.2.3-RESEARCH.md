## Mathematical / Engineering Framework For Invocation-Surface Archaeology

Treat the bundled runner as a partially observed state machine whose observable surface is the tuple:

`S = (binary hash, cwd, wrapper, mode token, task token, asset arguments, environment, stdout/stderr class, receipt class)`

The phase goal is not to infer semantics from residue alone. It is to partition the surviving surface into three classes:

1. live callable smoke surfaces
2. live callable non-smoke candidate surfaces
3. residue-only names or wrappers with no current live route

The decisive comparison is a falsification problem:

- starting negative fixed point: `Running T1: Contraction...`, `run_id = t1_contraction`, normalized receipt `d3e721...`
- historical positive witness family: `InterferenceTask: Detected JSON RegionTags.` and `G2 Boundary Readout Experiment (Mode: R2Contrastive)`

Archaeology succeeds when every plausible live surface around the bundled generation is either mapped to one of those classes or ruled out with receipts. It does not require recovering the family; exact retirement proof is an equally valid end state.
