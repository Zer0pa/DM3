## What Is Already Known

- The live device validator fails on the historical RM10 parity witness under
  default handling but passes under explicit hashes.
- The embedded binary strings already expose the stale documented `verify.json`
  hash family and the live validation wording around canonical hash matching.
- The top-level `/data/local/tmp/dm3_runner` binary is not behaviorally
  equivalent to the legacy `/data/local/tmp/dm3/dm3_runner` surface.
- The top-level root family still stalls after resonance start even when the
  missing ambient `RegionTags_v1.bin` gate is cleared.

## Required Learnings

1. The smallest exact explanation or repair for the validator-default rule.
2. The smallest exact explanation or repair for the top-level root-family
   stall.
3. Whether the official hardened `F2` outlier packet can run on the top-level
   family without legacy substitution.
4. Whether one narrow heterogeneous split can be retained on the same family
   without inflating the authority ceiling.

## Anti-Proxy Checks

- Do not let a strings-only clue replace a behaviorally confirmed rule.
- Do not let a cleanroom or copied-layout experiment become silent legacy
  substitution.
- Do not treat blocked packets as outlier evidence.
- Do not widen from one narrow heterogeneous split into a general GPU or NPU
  success story.
