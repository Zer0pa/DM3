  remains a support, debugging, compilation, and comparison lane.

  manifold, field computer, or recovered glory object.

  1. write the internal execution pack
  2. engineer the Red Magic system
  3. mine the older histories
  4. stand up the testing surfaces

  6. step back and decide what the object seems to be
