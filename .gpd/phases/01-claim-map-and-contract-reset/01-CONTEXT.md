## Required Outputs

- a source-linked `Txx` registry update
- an explicit boundary between recoverable baseline claims and unrecovered hybrid claims
- a narrowed restart battery that can later run on both Mac host and RM10 Pro
- a branch register distinguishing true hypothesis branches from batteries and parameter sweeps
- a battery hierarchy covering micro-batteries, medium batteries, and long batteries across device lanes

## Non-Goals

- proving the manifesto true from prose
- treating the image stack as scientific evidence
- rebuilding HRM, GPU, scar-engine, or micro-transformer layers before the baseline is replayed

## Stop And Rethink Conditions

- most `Txx` claims cannot be traced to any recovered artifact
- the recovered October or Genesis baseline no longer replays deterministically
- the restart battery depends on missing hybrid source rather than the recoverable substrate

## Working Rule

If a claim cannot be traced to code, a protocol, a ledger, or a reproducible artifact, it does not count as established evidence in the restart.
