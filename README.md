# DM3

## What This Is
DM3 is a graph-structure diagnostic built around a fixed 380-vertex C3-symmetric graph and a compiled 72,960-dimensional relaxation system. The strongest mirrored Session 7 result is a five-arm substrate-null on the harmonic dynamics layer, alongside a stable default fingerprint for the self-evaluating six-gate campaign. DM3 is instrument-shaped rather than codec-shaped, so the parser-required identity rows below are used as product tags for this diagnostic surface.

## What We Prove
- DM3 is a compiled structural diagnostic on a fixed 380-vertex C3-symmetric graph with a 72,960-dimensional relaxation state.
- The `harmonic` task exhibits reproducible HIGH and LOW basins, with IID HIGH-basin selection at approximately 34% on the promoted packet and overlapping Wilson confidence intervals across five mirrored Session 7 substrate arms.
- The Session 7 smoke receipt fixes a default `exp_r1_r4_campaign` reproducibility fingerprint at canonical SHA `9006df4ec02c8872...` on the `--steps 5` surface.
- `RandomAdj_v1.bin` flips R1, `RegionTags_v2.bin` flips R2, and the mirrored partial Session 7 steps sweep leaves `R3 == false` with `operational_steps` capped at 10 on the SY-default surface.
- Twelve `--task` values are callable on the promoted binary surface.

## What We Don't Claim
- DM3 is not a portfolio-wide platform component; it stands alone as its own product.
- DM3 is not an AI system, and transformer-created bistability is not a live claim.
- Rotational angle `60°` does not uniquely break C3; Claim gamma remains retracted.
- No external competitive benchmark pack is promoted on this root surface yet.
- The full 40-invocation Session 7 gate-layer battery is not fully mirrored in this checkout yet, so the broader gate-null claim remains a sync item rather than a promoted proof anchor here.
- The Session 7 truth-sensor sweep and final combined-axis `steps=100` gate cell are not promoted on this root surface yet.
